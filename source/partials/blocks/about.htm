Our Legacy

Hi there, my name is Raiden Sakura.

I'm the founder of Shogun. It all started when I was a staff for my Honkai Impact 3 Armada Discord server called Teriri's Gaming Hub. After some time I realize we needed a way to forward messages sent in one channel to another server automatically, and thus in my search of finding a self-hostable open source bot which has that feature, I stumbled upon Red Discord Bot.

Red Discord Bot

Language: Python

Red is a fully modular bot – meaning all features and commands can be enabled/disabled to your liking, making it completely customizable. This is a self-hosted bot – meaning you will need to host and maintain your own instance. You can turn Red into an admin bot, music bot, trivia bot, new best friend or all of these together!

The bot came with a few basic default plugins (called cogs), but due to their vast open-source community of various people creating their own cogs, I found one made by coffeebank.

Coffeebank: One of the cog creators for Red Discord bot which made the feature I wanted possible, thanks to their msgmover cog. Check out their GitHub profile here!

After some time configuring the bot (which now called Shogun), I decided it's worth it to open her up to be invited by others since I find her sets of advanced tools something useful and unique that rarely any other bot has.

I gradually upgraded the system she runs on to make sure she's able to perform tasks smoothly, moving between multiple hosting provider. At the time of writing, she now runs on 2 vCore Ryzen 9 5950X with 4GB RAM for a discounted amount of $9 monthly.

As Discord bots needs to be officially verified before being able to be in more than 100 servers at once, I do not plan on mass-advertising Shogun or increasing her popularity. Verifying Shogun would require me stripping some of her most useful features in order to follow Discord verification guideline, and that goes against my wish and my initial philosophy of creating Shogun, which is to cater to niche and more specific needs.

If you find Shogun useful to you and wish to contribute, helping fund the $9 monthly hosting cost is already enough for us. Make sure to join our Discord server before contributing on our Ko-fi page to obtain a special role. Your Discord tag will also be added into our /supporters command.
